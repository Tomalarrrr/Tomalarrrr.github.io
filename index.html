  • How Does MoonBlast Work?

    The MoonBlast Token operates via a tax protocol that aims to provide support for the life of the project.

    No.1 Rocket Insurnace Fund

    This is a wallet, visible to all, that provides critical price support during bearish trends. Insurnace funds are community driven, meaning they're used when the community feel it will benefit the projects journey.

    No.2 Rocket Treasury

    Our Rocket Treasury Wallet, which too is visible to all, hold funds in BNB/BUSD that are used for Marketing and Project Payements. It's also used for promotional activities to attract new investors and further benefit investors.

    No.3 Diamond Hand Wallet

    Next up is the Diamond Hand Wallet. This is the dead wallet ending 0x0000000000000000000dEaD. A % of all tax is sent to this wallet in our MoonBlast Tokens. As its name suggests, this wallet is dead to all and not accessible - therefore meaning this wallet will forever grow & rebase, ensuring a floor price is always there for investors as a security measure.

    No.4 Auto Liquidity

    Finally the Auto-liquidity function. This is a % of the tax from each transaction sent to the liquidity pool for when investors take some profits! Keeping it topped up means theres plenty for everyone. Remember liquidty is key!
