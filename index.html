  • How Does
    MoonBlast
    Work?

    The MoonBlast Token operates via a tax protocol that aims to provide support for the life of the project.

    No.1 Rocket Insurnace Fund

    This is a wallet, visible to all, that provides price support during bearish markets. Insurnace funds are community controlled, meaning they're used when the community feel it will benefit the projects journey

    No.2 Rocket Treasury

    Our Rocket Treasury Wallet, which too is visible to all, hold funds that are used for marketing and repayements. It's also used for promotional Airdrops and other functions to benefit investors

    No.3 Diamond Hand Wallet

    Next up is the Diamond Hand Wallet. This is a dead wallet ending in 0x00000000000DeAd. A % of all tax is sent to this wallet in the form of MoonBlast Tokens. As its name suggested this wallet is dead and not accessable! This means that this wallet will grow & rebase, ensuring a floor price is always there for investors as a security measure.

    No.4 Auto Liquidity

    Finally the Auto-liquidity function. This is a % of the tax from each transaction sent to the liquidity pool for when investors take some profits! Keeping it topped up means theres plenty for everyone. Liquidty is key!
